Good _____. Thanks for dropping by. I'm Tricia and I develop and design web-based applications in New York City. Before all this, I taught full-time at a number of elementary and middle schools in high needs neighborhoods. Feeling overwhelmed and annoyed with the current state and foreseable future of the classroom experience, I ventured off into the world of technology with a plan for improving it. As a means of market research, I substitute teach within New York City's Department of Education. I get to see first-hand the strategies and tools that work, and bring attention to those that do not. Read my journal to learn more about my experiences. My side projects include designing and building tools to improve these spaces for both teachers and students.
